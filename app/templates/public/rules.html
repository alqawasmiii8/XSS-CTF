Competition Rules

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse.

General Conduct

  • Do not attack the CTF infrastructure (this website, database, scoring system).
  • Do not generate excessive load (no brute-forcing flags or endpoints unless explicitly instructed).
  • Do not try to DoS/DDoS the platform or challenges.
  • Do not share flags or solutions with other teams before the event concludes.
  • Respect other participants and the organizers.

Eligibility

Anyone is free to play, but prize eligibility may be restricted to specific groups (e.g., students, residents of specific regions). Check the official announcements for details.

Flag Format

Unless stated otherwise in the challenge description, all flags will follow the format:

XSS{some_random_string_here_1234}

Disqualification

Violation of these rules will result in immediate disqualification and a ban from future events hosted on this platform. Organizers' decisions are final.
