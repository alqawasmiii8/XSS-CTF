🛡️ Fair Play & Anticheat Rules

To maintain a competitive and fair environment, we have strict rules against cheating.

1. No Flag Sharing

Sharing flags, solutions, or highly specific hints with other users or teams is strictly prohibited. Flags are uniquely generated or tracked; if someone else submits your flag, both accounts will be permanently banned.

2. Multi-Accounting is Banned

You are allowed only one active account per person. Creating "smurf" accounts, fake accounts to test payloads, or accounts to unlock hints without losing points on your main team is cheating. Our system monitors IP addresses and device fingerprints.

3. Brute-Forcing & Automated Scanners

Do not use automated vulnerability scanners (like Nessus, Acunetix) or directory brute-forcers (DirBuster) unless a challenge explicitly permits it. Rate limits are in place, and aggressive automated traffic will result in an automatic IP ban.

4. Do Not Attack the Infrastructure

Your targets are the challenges, not the CTF platform itself. Any attempt to exploit the scoreboard, database, or server infrastructure will lead to immediate disqualification and possible legal action.

Penalties for Cheating

Any violation of these rules will result in the immediate and unappealable removal of your team from the scoreboard and a permanent ban from future events.

Play fair. Hack hard.
