Frequently Asked Questions

What is a CTF?

Capture The Flag (CTF) is a cybersecurity competition where participants solve security challenges to find hidden "flags" and earn points.

Is there a team size limit?

Yes, the maximum team size is 4 members. You can also participate solo.

Do I need to be an expert?

Not at all! We have challenges ranging from beginner-friendly to extremely difficult. It is a great place to learn.

What happens if I find a bug in the platform?

Please report it directly to the admins via Discord or Email. Do NOT exploit it to gain points or disrupt the competition.
